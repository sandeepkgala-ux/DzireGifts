# First-Generation Guidance

You are working on the **first generation** of this app. The monorepo you were given is a blank template — `apps/web` currently renders a placeholder `HomePage` ("hello") at `/`. Your job is to turn that template INTO the app the user just asked for.

**Ship a WOW first impression.** The first load must feel finished, memorable, and intentional — never a wireframe with placeholder sections. The visitor's first reaction should be "this looks great," not "this works."

**Bring the page to life — but vary how, every build.** Read the brief, then pick 2–4 craft moves from the categories below that fit *this* world. Never apply the same combo twice in a row. A quiet editorial brief earns one elegant marker underline; a playful consumer app earns a marquee, tilted cards, and a confetti reveal. Pick what serves the brief — leave the rest out.

- **Kinetic typography** — animated headlines, letter reveals, hand-drawn marker/highlight strokes under a key phrase, scribble accents, large display type with subtle weight or color shifts
- **Moving strips** — horizontal marquees (news-ticker style), scrolling logo ribbons, color-line tickers, repeating word loops, infinite carousels
- **Depth & 3D feel** — subtle tilt on hover, parallax photography, perspective transforms, floating cards with offset shadows, layered z-stacks, isometric tableaus
- **Decorative graphics** — color bars, accent strokes, halftone grain, blob shapes, gradient ribbons, hand-drawn doodles, geometric accent lines, sticker-style badges
- **Scroll & cursor interactions** — scroll-triggered reveals, magnetic buttons, cursor-aware accents, smooth section transitions, sticky split scrolls

Keep motion **purposeful and performant** — never gratuitous. Animate `transform` and `opacity` only, respect `prefers-reduced-motion`, and isolate heavy effects to small components. Follow `design/SKILL.md` for craft execution and page composition.

(If this file is NOT in your context, you are on a later iteration — follow the normal rules and ignore this doc.)
